Future of data science

Across the world, everyday activities are generating tons of data, and these data are collected in this way that we use all information with this data. The insights that can be generated from this data are very valuable in making informed choices. It can be as simple a choice as ordering food online to something as complex as identifying the right personal finance options.


Qualified data scientists & data engineers are the need of the hour, considering the huge demand for them in the current job market. Anyone can learn data science, you don't have to be from a technical background to understand the techniques involved in this field. Domain generated in any sector plus knowledge of data science will help professionals understand patterns among the data generated. A huge amount of job opportunities are available in this field and hence they are investing heavily in creating their data teams. Data Science is one of the fastest-growing sectors that is predicted to created 11.5 million jobs by 2026, companies across the various sector have understood the importance of data science. This has created new job opportunities in large volumes. New job roles like chief Data officier have started coming up in the

The World Economic Forum predicts that by 2022, data analysts and scientists will be the number one emerging job in the world. New start-ups are coming up based on Data Science, Venture Capitalists and Investors are keen on investing in new companies that offer data science services and solutions. India has the 3rd largest ecosystem for Al & Data Sciences enabled companies making it a huge market for potential inventors. Data Science is a very unique and fun domain, data-driven business solutions help organizations make informed choices, thereby removing any potentials risks. Understanding the reasoning behind the success of a particular business decision that is backed by numbers opens up various perspectives and new ways to look at the concept of solutioning.

Online Education : “ New Normal ”

Corona virus outbreak has made us realized that unpredictable is the new predictable , uncommon is new common and moreover for students Online education is “New Normal”.

Those times are long gone when staying at home would be your escape from studies , Now knowledge is feed through webcams and computers. If analyzed there is nothing wrong with it in lieu it is proving to be very beneficial for students to utilize their time from comfort of their homes.

But then why we still prefer offline education ? Why numerous students find online class boring? How to pretend to attend an online class?



These are some of statement/heading/question which I find amusing and concerning while gathering information on Online education.
Moreover , this blog is a fusion of what we think and what we should really think ! And here by We , I mean I and my friends and their friends and some other acquaintance.

What we think ! Aka Negatives of Online Education.



Online course require excellent time management skill and armoured will towards studies which only a fraction of teenagers is equipped. This ennui from education when combed with online classes can make you completely uninterested in studies. It becomes arduous to focus and process the knowledge aimed to brighten our future. Moreover , students tend to mute class and take a quick nap or join class right after teacher says , “Attendance Time”. It is hard to get motivated and online classes for long duration can successfully make you unsuccessful because of nurtured poor concentration.

Apart from difficulty to concentrate , Online Education creates a sense of isolation which might be the worst phase for a growing individual. Mental health is looked down in standard Indian parenting syllabus but , it is something of great significance and very imperative for overall development of a healthy character. Some students prefer extra curricular over academics and school/college is the best place to learn and accomplish your passion with an extra backup of education whereas it’s not the case with online education. Believe me you can’t dance or even sing in a online class and if you plan to do it someday Good Luck !

This point might not be relatable to everyone but underprivileged students are hit hardest after everything is online. Not everyone has the same device or high speed internet to attend an online class. Not every boy can make a 20 page presentation when his father needs him at fields after class. Not every girl can attend all the classes when she has to cook and clean along with her mother. Concept of online education lacks empathy towards underprivileged students when they are paying full fee for their respective course.

What we should really think ? aka Positives of online education.



Online education comes with every advantage that an individual can imagine just by hearing the word “Technology”.
Lectures can be recorded and later revised whenever you want , wherever you want ! Rewind them , skip them or delete them , you own your education and can grasp it at your pace. It bestows you freedom which is the ultimate goal for any teenager. Before exam week , just put your teacher on loop and grab every knowledge aimed to brighten your future. DejaVu?

I am not an introvert but , I’m sure there are many among us not able to raise hand even knowing the solution in a busy class of 84 students. While in the online class one can simply post an answer in chat box or present their notebook to share their piece of knowledge. A sense of achievement is felt which is sometimes enough to make your day. And If you have big mouth , then you can have a healthy debate with your classmate or teacher which always brings clarity on the respective topic.

I cannot help myself not to include this bounty “Online Exam”. It is said a piece of paper cannot determine your future but , recently the companies offering internships are whispering “ at least 7 CGPA or do marketing “ . Online exams can help you achieve better marks as one can focus extensively at their comfortable place or of course cheating is a lot easier if you are online. I know this point is controversial because if everyone is intelligent on paper then how can quality students be classified differently. But not every studious kid is intelligent and vice versa. For e.g. A quality Engineer is someone who can figure things out not someone who knows things. When you start working for a firm they train you before you actually work and this is the time a true learner understands the job and execute it. Online exams in long run can remove this distinction of superiority based on marks and can value the true potential of a student. Everyone deserves to be tested before rejection .
